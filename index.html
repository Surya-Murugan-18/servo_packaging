About

Established in 1996, Servo Packaging Limited, an ISO 9001:2008 certified company, has emerged as a global pioneer in the plastic and packaging field over the past two decades. We concentrate our diligence and expertise to manufacture and supply masterbatches of high-grade quality and competitive pricing that are delivered on time. We hold thriving business relations across the globe, with numerous satisfied clients in India, Africa, East Europe, Latin and South America, and Southeast Asia.

Servo Packaging Limited is the authorized Indian distributor of Polystar Machinery - an internationally renowned manufacturer of plastic recycling, blown film extrusion, and sealing machines in Taiwan.
